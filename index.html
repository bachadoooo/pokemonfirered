About Cookie Clicker

1504 votes 4/5

Cookie Clicker is an idle clicker game. This game is quite popular as it is suitable for all ages. Play this game for free, no ads, and no sign in required.

Bake millions of delicious cookies by clicking on a giant cookie. You can play the cookie clicker game on web browsers, iPad, iPhone, Samsung, Android, and Windows Phone. Cookie Clicker is developed by Ortiel.

Options: 
- Auto clicks one every 10 seconds
- Has full-screen play mode.
- SAVE: Game will autosave every 60s or use hotkey to save the game: Ctrl + S
- EXPORT SAVE: This feature helps the player to back up so the player can copy it to another computer.
- IMPORT SAVE: Use hotkey to import save Ctrl + 0
- SAVE TO FILE
- LOAD FROM FILE: This feature helps the player to back up your game into a file on your computer. 
- WIPE SAVE: Remove all game data( upgrades, cookies, achievements,..)

Features:

- Smooth graphics
- Amazing sound

Wake and bake 1 cookie with your mother or dad or friend, etc,.! Making some dough and bake 1,000 cookies in one ascension.

- You become a World-famous bakery by baking 1 billion cookies,
- You become a Cosmic bakery by baking 100 billion cookies in one ascension.
- You become a Galactic bakery by baking 1 trillion cookies,
- You become a Universal bakery by baking 100 trillion cookies in one ascension.
- You become a Timeless bakery by baking 1 quadrillion cookies,
- You become an Infinite bakery by baking 100 quadrillion cookies,
- You become an Immortal bakery by baking 1 quintillion cookies in one ascension.
